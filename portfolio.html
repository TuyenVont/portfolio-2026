Project 01

Odoo ERP
Project Lead for SME Fruit Business

Project Lead Odoo 19 ERP Planning Custom Add-ons BPMN

Role: Team Leader · Timeline: 2-month ERP implementation project

Context: The project focused on implementing ERP for a small-to-medium fruit purchasing and retail business. The business struggled with manual purchasing, inventory tracking, quality control, daily price changes, fruit wastage, and slow operational reporting.

My Responsibility: As the team leader, I planned the project roadmap, assigned tasks to team members, coordinated implementation progress, and managed the flow from business analysis, BPMN process design, master data preparation, Odoo configuration, testing, and final demo.

Solution: My team implemented the end-to-end Odoo workflow for purchasing, inventory, sales, invoicing, payment, and reporting. We also developed 2 custom modules: fruit_fresh_operations for Lot/Batch, QC, FEFO and wastage log; and fruit_crm_daily_pricing for CRM market signals and daily fruit price boards.

Result: Completed a working ERP demo on Odoo with custom modules and implementation documentation so the team could present the project logic, business value, and system outcome clearly.

Project 02

SEO Website
& Project Management Report for langdhhcm.info.vn

SEO Content · Looker Studio · PMBOK · WBS · AON

SEO WordPress Looker Studio Project Management WBS AON

Role: UI Contributor · SEO Content Writer · Project Management Contributor

Context: This project focused on building and optimizing langdhhcm.info.vn, an informational website for students around the VNUHCM University Village area. The website provided content about dormitories, food, student life, local services, and useful locations.

My Responsibility: I contributed to the website interface, wrote SEO articles, supported on-page content structure, and created a performance report using Looker Studio. I also participated in the E-commerce Project Management report, including project planning, WBS, AON, quality testing criteria, KPI/SLA, and report formatting.

Solution: Built a student-oriented SEO website, planned keyword groups, published optimized content, monitored Google Search Console data through Looker Studio, and documented the project management plan following a PMBOK-based structure.

Result: The website recorded 621 impressions, 35 clicks, 5.64% CTR, and an average position of 27.67 during the reporting period. The project also delivered a complete management document covering scope, timeline, WBS, AON, quality control, KPI/SLA, and project measurement.
